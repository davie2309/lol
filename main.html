For My Lil Muse

You've brought so much color into my life, turning the ordinary into something extraordinary over the past few months. Thank you for being the artist who painted joy and warmth onto my canvas. Your kindness is like a comforting garden, and I can't thank you enough for making my world brighter and more beautiful

You're as vital to me as the sun and the moon. Like the sun brightens the day and the moon enchants the night, your presence lights up my world. In the cosmic dance of life, you're my constant, and I can't thank you enough for being my shining light.
